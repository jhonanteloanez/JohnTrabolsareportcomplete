# 📊 Nicotine Pouches: Sales, Inventory & Customer Analytics

## 🎯 Project Objective
The goal of this Business Intelligence project is to analyze the sales performance, inventory health, and customer behavior for a nicotine pouches business in Santa Cruz. The dashboard provides actionable insights to optimize stock levels and understand digital channel performance.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **Microsoft Power BI:** Data visualization and dashboard design.
- **Power Query:** Data cleaning, transformation, and manipulation.
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Advanced measure creation (Current Stock, AOV, Retention Rate).
- **Data Modeling:** Star schema (Fact & Dimension tables).

## 💡 Key Business Insights

1. **Revenue & Product Performance:** 
   Total revenue reached Bs 248,606 with a healthy 34.3% profit margin. *Citrus 11mg* is the undisputed top performer, generating nearly double the revenue of the lowest-performing product.
   
2. **Inventory Risk (Critical Finding):**
   The Inventory Risk Matrix reveals a critical imbalance: *Citrus 11mg* (highest sales) has one of the lowest stock levels, risking a stock-out. Conversely, *Apple Mint* has high inventory (>370 units) but lower sales volume, trapping capital.

3. **Customer Behavior & Channels:**
   Customer retention is strong, consistently staying above 60%. Geographically, *Equipetrol* and *Las Palmas* drive the most revenue. The primary acquisition channels are Instagram and Direct Web traffic.
